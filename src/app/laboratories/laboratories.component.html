HVAC Resource Map for Laboratories

What is this resource?

The HVAC Resource Map for Laboratories is an intuitive graphical interface that provides quick access to a broad array of quality information on design, operations and maintenance best practices, and energy and water efficiency measures in single-pass HVAC systems that are 100% outside air and do not have return air.

The resources cover the central plant, distribution systems, and zone systems. The primary audiences are facility managers, operations staff, and design engineers who want to improve central plant and distribution efficiency.

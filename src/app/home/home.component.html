Looking to learn about HVAC systems?

This website offers quick access to an array of information related to commercial and laboratory HVAC system design, operations, and maintenance best practices. The resources cover central plants, distributions systems, and zone systems, with specific guidance for laboratories.

This resource is designed for facility managers, operations staff, and design engineers who want to understand and improve commercial HVAC system efficiency. It provides introductions and definitions for HVAC systems and components with extensive links for additional information about technologies, energy efficiency measures, trainings, procurement, and case studies.
